Program description

When you tap "Watch this car" on a participating dealership's website and provide your mobile number, you are enrolling to receive automated text alerts about the vehicle(s) you choose to watch. These alerts may include a sign-up confirmation, price-drop notifications, demand updates, and sold notices.

How to opt in

You opt in by tapping "Watch this car" on a vehicle detail page and entering your mobile phone number. By doing so, you consent to receive automated marketing and informational text messages about that vehicle from LotPulse on behalf of the dealership. Consent is not a condition of any purchase.

Message frequency

Message frequency varies based on the vehicles you watch and their pricing activity. You will typically receive a confirmation message at sign-up and occasional messages thereafter when there is a relevant update.

Cost

Message and data rates may apply. LotPulse does not charge for the alerts, but your mobile carrier's standard messaging and data rates may apply based on your plan.

How to opt out

You can cancel at any time by replying STOP to any message. After you send STOP, we will send a single confirmation message and you will not receive further messages unless you sign up again.

Help

For help, reply HELP to any message, or contact us at scott@lotpulse.io.

Supported carriers

The program is available on major U.S. wireless carriers. Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.

Privacy

Your information is handled in accordance with our Privacy Policy. We do not sell your personal information, and we do not share your mobile number or SMS opt-in consent with third parties for their own marketing.
