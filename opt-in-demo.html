How shoppers opt in to LotPulse text alerts

This page shows the exact "Watch this car" widget as it appears on a participating dealership's vehicle detail page, calling the live LotPulse API. Use it to verify the opt-in flow, consent language, and checkbox behavior for SMS campaign review.

The opt-in flow, step by step

  1. A shopper browsing a vehicle detail page sees the "Watch this car" widget and taps the main button.
  2. A bottom sheet slides up with a field for their mobile number (and an optional first-name field).
  3. Below the phone field is an optional, unchecked-by-default SMS consent checkbox. The exact disclosure text shown to the shopper reads:

    Consent checkbox — as it appears in the widget
    OPTIONAL
    Also text me when the price drops. I agree to receive automated marketing text messages about this vehicle from this dealer at the number above. Consent is not a condition of purchase.
    Msg & data rates may apply. Msg frequency varies. Reply STOP to opt out, HELP for help.
  4. The "Start watching" submit button is never disabled by the checkbox. It requires only a valid US phone number. Checking the box is not required to submit — this is by design, to avoid forced-consent (error 30923).
    Two separate outcomes depending on checkbox state: If the box is checked: a demand-signal watch is created AND the shopper is enrolled for SMS alerts. They receive a confirmation text immediately after submitting.

    If the box is unchecked: the watch is still created as a demand signal for the dealer. No texts are ever sent to this shopper unless they return and opt in separately on a future visit.
  5. Every outbound text message ends with opt-out instructions. Replying STOP immediately unsubscribes; replying HELP returns support contact info (scott@lotpulse.io).

Required disclosures

The consent checkbox explicitly states:

  1. That the shopper is consenting to receive automated marketing text messages about the specific vehicle
  2. That consent is not a condition of purchase
  3. That message and data rates may apply
  4. That they can reply STOP to cancel at any time
  5. That they can reply HELP for help

The checkbox is unchecked by default and checking it is never required to use the "Watch this car" feature.
