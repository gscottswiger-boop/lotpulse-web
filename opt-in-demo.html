How shoppers opt in to LotPulse text alerts

This page shows the exact "Watch this car" widget as it appears on a participating dealership's vehicle page, calling our live API. Use it to verify the opt-in flow and consent language for SMS campaign review.

The opt-in flow, step by step

  1. A shopper browsing a dealer's vehicle detail page sees the widget below and taps "Watch this car."
  2. A bottom sheet opens asking for their mobile number, with the consent disclosure shown directly above the submit button (visible below).
  3. Tapping "Start watching" submits the opt-in. The shopper immediately receives a confirmation text.
  4. Going forward, that shopper receives a text only if the vehicle's price drops or it sells.
  5. Every message includes opt-out instructions. Replying STOP unsubscribes immediately; HELP returns support contact info.

Required disclosures

The consent screen explicitly states:

  1. That the shopper is consenting to receive automated text messages about the specific vehicle
  2. That consent is not a condition of purchase
  3. That message and data rates may apply
  4. That they can reply STOP to cancel at any time
